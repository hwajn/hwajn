Xpace Cultural Centre—8" x 10" art book (214pg), 300ct
Hired as graph­ic des­ig­ner, I was sing­ly resp­onsib­le for the art direct­ion, des­ign, & ex­ecut­ion of a full-­colour, print­ed art bo­ok. The res­ult is a perf­ect bo­und & gl­ossy-covered, 214 page bo­ok, with dimen­sions of 8" x 10". A bat­ch of 300 un­its was print­ed.
Tasks included research & thematic development, layout, typography, composing, & print coordination. This book's purpose is to commemorate artists & writers exhibited at Xpace from '20-'21. The exhibited year has experienced a continuous year of pandemic life, heavily influencing a worldwide comeback of DIY culture. Resonating with 70s punk culture where creative DIY methods were an economic necessity, '20-'21 was a time of making do with what one had, thus inspiring a VOLUME related to cut & paste, & the economical tatics of punk zine making. Subtle, inspired elements included images treated with roughly-cut borders & ball pen digital brushes created for underlines. Yellow highlighter marker lines created from digitally treated marker scans, were used to highlight text citations in a book dotted with interesting references. The result is an expressive & subtle art book that proudly displays the work of Xpace's exhibited creatives.
