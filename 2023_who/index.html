Hwa-Jin Jun
b. '94
(b. '94) 
is currently a designer at aftermodern.lab (circa 2020). In 2022 she was appointed as the Head of Marketing at the non-profit organization, Korean Canadian Scholarship Foundation (KCSF). She is based in New York City (USA), and Toronto (Canada).
Prior to her life in design, Hwa-Jin graduated from Queen’s U with a bachelor of science and worked at the GTA GSW office, a pharmaceutical advertising company. Here she was responsible for clients such as Tylenol and Polysporin. Unsatisfied with this career path, Hwa-Jin resolved to pursue her lifetime love of art and enrolled at OCAD U. Together, her scientific education, inquisitive nature, and creative tendencies weave together as a unique perspective on design.
Hwa-Jin currently works with a variety of clientele—from commercial/technological companies to non-profit art organizations. She is experienced and knowledgeable in book and print design, web design, accessible design, art direction, and marketing.
