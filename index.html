Hwa‑Jin Jun is a visual designer born in the year of the dog. She is a consummate hiking am­ateur, and enjoys spending her time out­doors ten­ding to her budding backyard veg­etable garden. At her core, she is a tenacious explorer and constant learner; Hwa‑Jin adeptly translates her unabashed curiosity and uncon­ventional style into her practice involving inter­active digital exper­iences and process-driven, collage-like designs.
