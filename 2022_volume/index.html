Xpace Cultural Centre—8" x 10" art book (214pg), 300ct
I sing­ly art direct­ed, des­igned, & ex­ecut­ed a full-­colour, physical art bo­ok. The res­ult is a perf­ect bo­und & gl­ossy-covered, 214 page bo­ok, with dimen­sions of 8" x 10". A bat­ch of 300 un­its was print­ed. ...The purpose of this book is to commemorate artists & writers exhibited at Xpace during '20-'21. Resonating with 70s punk culture, '20-'21 was a time of making do with what one had—thus inspiring my art direction with the quirks of punk-zine making. Subtle design elements within the book include images treated with roughly-cut borders & ball pen digital brushes created for underlines. Yellow highlighter lines made from digitally-treated marker scans were also created to highlight text citations in a book dotted with interesting references. The result is an expressive yet clean art book that proudly displays the work of Xpace's exhibited creatives in a culturally relevant environment.
