Design TO—digital & print ads, awards, & gifs
As the current jun­ior des­ig­ner at aftermodern.lab, I was ta­sk­ed wi­th indep­endent­ly dev­elop­ing gra­ph­ic assets for the Design TO Festival—a non-profit arts org­aniz­ation & event series in Toronto. ...These pieces include a bus shel­ter light box ad, win­dow ad, web ads, soc­ial media gifs, & award plac­ards (a selection shown). The given art dir­ect­ion was lim­ited to colour pal­ette & client req­uire­ments. Incl­uding type­sett­ing & laying out the var­ious logos & text as per my des­ign, I was also resp­ons­ible for ideat­ing & illust­rating the playful vis­ual back­drops of the web ads, aw­ard plac­ards, & social media gifs.
