Personal—digital poster & interface concept
A 24" x 36" full-colour print poster. Remove the paper cutouts and assemble my personal daily use items of the pandemic to vaguely live as I do.
With the pandemic requiring home isolation, my daily-use items have changed in response. These objects are translated into 2D forms that can be assembled into 3D paper duplicates. In recreating these items, the audience can attempt to quarantine as I do. With their artefact-riddled textures, simplified geometry, & the inevitable mistakes of assembly, the resulting paper models will vaguely resemble their original form. Created with Blender, photography, & Unfolder.
