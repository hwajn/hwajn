self-driven—24" x 36" print poster
A 24" x 36" full-colour print poster. Remove the paper cutouts & assemble my ‘every day carry’ of the pandemic to vaguely live as I do. ...With the pandemic requiring home isolation, my every day carry (edc; a personal collection of useful objects one carries outside the home) has changed in response. My edc is translated into 2D forms that can be assembled into 3D paper duplicates. In recreating my items, the audience can attempt to quarantine as I do. Because of their artefact-riddled textures, simplified geometry, & inevitable mistakes of assembly, the resulting paper models vaguely resemble their original form, just as present life only poorly imitates pre-pandemic living. Created with Blender, photography, & Unfolder.
